Info

This page

This website helps you search for songs and artists and then play a small part of the selected song. If you select "lyrics" under the "Play song" tab - you can also see the lyrics of these songs.

Under the "Generator" tab, you can get a random song, it's name and artist with just one click!

The website has combined several open API's that provide it with information about the music. Sometimes it can be a bit clumsy in its fetching and a double click or refresh may be in order!
